About Me

I am a speech and language engineer, with broad experience of the IT industry, from my background as a technology consultant.

Technology is a powerful tool that is rapidly changing our world. I want to use technology to build a future that is inclusive: I am interested in preventing unintentional harms caused by new technologies, and ensuring we consider the needs of a diverse group of users. I am at my best when collaborating with a team, learning new things, and challenging the status quo.
